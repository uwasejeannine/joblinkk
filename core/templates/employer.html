Tangiza Urugendo Rwo Kubona Abakozi Babereye Ikigo Cyawe

Dufasha abakoresha kubona abakozi b'umwuga. Sangiza imyanya y'akazi yawe kuri urubuga rwacu kandi ubone abakozi babereye akazi kawe.

Tanga Amakuru y'Umukoresha Reba Abakozi

Ibyo Ukeneye Nk'umukoresha

Serivisi zinoze zo gufasha abakoresha kubona abakozi babereye ibigo byabo

01

Shyigikira Abakozi

Dufasha abakoresha kubona abakozi bafite ubumenyi buhamye, bashobora guteza imbere umusaruro no guteza imbere umuco mwiza w'akazi.

02

Kwamamaza Amahirwe

Sangiza imyanya y'akazi yawe ku bantu benshi kugira ngo ukurure abakandida b'icyitegererezo kandi b'impano.

03

Abakandida Basuzumwe

Dusuzuma abakandida byimbitse kugira ngo tugaragaze gusa abafite ubumenyi n'ubunararibonye bihuye n'ibikenewe byawe.

04

Ibisubizo Bihinduka

Hindura uburyo bwo gukoresha abakozi bwawe kugira ngo buhuze n'ibikenewe by'ubucuruzi bihindagurika.

Kutwiga Biratuma Ubona Abakozi Babereye

Mu gihe cy'imyaka 25+ y'uburambe, twahaye abakoresha benshi abakozi babereye. Duha agaciro gukorera hamwe n'abakoresha kugira ngo tumve neza ibikenewe kandi tubafashe kubona abakozi bakwiye.

Gufasha abakoresha kugabanya igihe n'amafaranga bijyana no gushaka abakozi

Gufasha abakozi kwihutisha kubona akazi gahuje n'ubushobozi bwabo

Tanga Amakuru y'Umukoresha

Serivisi Zacu

Dufasha abakoresha kubona abakozi b'umwuga binyuze muri izi serivisi

Gushaka Abakozi

Dushakira abakoresha abakozi bashoboye kandi bafite ubumenyi n'impamyabushobozi zikenewe. Dusuzuma abakandida kugira ngo dutoranyemo abashoboye.

Soma Byinshi

Guhugura Abakozi

Dutegura gahunda zo guhugura abakozi zikurikije ibikenewe n'ikigo, bityo bigatuma abakozi bagira ubushobozi bukenewe kugira ngo barusheho kugira umusaruro.

Soma Byinshi

Guhuza Impano

Dukorera abakoresha igenzura ryimbitse kugira ngo tubahuze n'abakozi bafite impano zihuye n'ibikenewe n'ibyifuzo by'ikigo kugira ngo bagere ku ntego z'ubucuruzi.
