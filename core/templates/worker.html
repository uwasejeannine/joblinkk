Shakisha Akazi

Koresha urubuga rwacu rwo gushakisha ushake imyanya y'akazi ibihumbi itangwa n'abakoresha b'icyitegererezo.

02

Tanga Umwirondoro

04

Guhabwa Akazi

Urugendo rwo Gushaka Akazi

Dore intambwe z'ingenzi mu gushaka akazi ukoresheje JobLink

1

Kwiyandikisha

Tangira ushyire amakuru yawe muri sisitemu yacu binyuze mu kumenyekanisha imyirondoro yawe. Ni intambwe yoroshye kandi yihuse yo gutangira urugendo rwawe.

2

Gushaka Amahirwe

Nyuma yo kwiyandikisha, ushobora kureba imyanya y'akazi iri muri sisitemu kandi ukora ubushakashatsi ku bakoresha batandukanye.

3

Gusaba Akazi

Igihe ubonye umwanya ukwiye, ushobora kuwusabira hakoreshejwe sisitemu yacu yoroshye kandi ishobora gukoreshwa ku buryo bworoshye.

4

Kubonana n'Umukoresha

Umukoresha aramutse ashimye dosiye yawe, uzahamagarwa kugira ngo uze kubonana na we. Dushobora kugufasha kwitegura iyo nterview.
